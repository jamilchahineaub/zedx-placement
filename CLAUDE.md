## Camera geometry
Both cameras on a ring around subject S = (0,0,0).
Same height and radius for both cameras in every layout.
Cam A at azimuth 0 degrees, cam B at (0 + relative_azimuth) degrees.
Cameras aim at (0, 0, aim_height_m) — hip height, NOT the floor.
Tilt must be less than max_tilt_deg from experiment.yaml or layout is skipped.
Both camera poses computed by camera_rig.py, never hardcoded.
